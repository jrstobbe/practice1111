Runningbacks

Jamaal Williams

Jamaal Malik Williams was born April 3, 1995 and played college football at BYU. Williams was drafted by the Green Bay Packers in the forth round of the 2017 NFL Draft.

Aaron Jones

Aaron LaRae Jones was born December 2, 1994 and played college football at the University of Texas-El Paso. In 2018 Jones ran for 728 yards. Jones was drafted by the Green Bay Packers in the fifth round of the 2017 NFL Draft.

Dexter Williams

Dexter Williams was born January 6, 1997 and played college football at Notre Dame. Williams is from Winter Garden, FLorida and attended Olympia High School. He was drafted by the Green Bay Packers in the sixth round of the 2019 NFL Draft

Malcolm Johnson

Malcom Johnson was born August 11, 1992 and played college football at Mississippi State. Johnson is from Winter Tuscaloosa, Alabama and attended Northridge High School. He was drafted by the Cleveland Browns in the sixth round of the 2015 NFL Draft.

Tra Carson

Tra Carson was born October 24, 1992 and played college football at Oregan and the transfered to Texas A&M. He was signed by the Cincinnati Bengals after going undrafted in the 2016 NFL Draft
